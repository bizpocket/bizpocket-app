Frequently Asked Questions

Is BizPocket really free?

Yes! The Free plan includes all core features — sales, inventory, vendors, expenses, and reports — for 1 business, forever.

What does Pro include?

Pro adds unlimited businesses (up to 99+), automatic cloud sync, encrypted cloud backup, and priority support. It comes with a 1-month free trial.

Does the app work offline?

Absolutely. BizPocket is offline-first — all your data is stored locally and syncs to the cloud when you're connected (Pro only).

How is my data protected?

Your local database is encrypted with SQLCipher. Cloud backups are also encrypted. We never sell your data.

Can I export my data?

Yes — all reports can be exported to PDF or Excel directly from the app. You can also export your full data as a backup file.

Which languages are supported?

BizPocket supports English, Urdu, Arabic, and Hindi — with full RTL (right-to-left) support for Urdu and Arabic.
