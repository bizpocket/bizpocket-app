All-in-One Business Hub

Manage everything from one dashboard

Forget juggling spreadsheets. BizPocket gives you a real-time overview of your business — every sale, expense, and account balance at a glance.

  • Dashboard shows Today / Weekly / Monthly metrics
  • Quick Actions for Sales, Items, Vendors, Expenses & Accounts
  • Recent sales feed with customer & invoice details
  • Cash in Hand and profit tracked automatically
Insightful Reports

Track profit & loss in real time

Make smarter decisions with live analytics. Filter by day, week, month, or a custom date range — then export to PDF or Excel with one tap.

  • Net Revenue, Gross Profit, and Cash in Hand at the top
  • Detailed Sales, Purchase, Expense & Inventory reports
  • Visual charts and trend graphs built in
  • Export to PDF and Excel — share with anyone
Vendor Management

Know exactly what you owe — and who you've paid

Keep your supplier relationships organised. Log every purchase, track balances, and see your full transaction history per vendor at any time.

  • Store vendor name, phone, and contact info
  • Log purchases with item, quantity & price per unit
  • Track payments and outstanding balances
  • Full purchase + payment history per supplier
Sync, Secure & Scale

Your data is safe, encrypted, and always backed up

SQLCipher-encrypted local storage, automatic cloud backup with Pro, and Google Sign-In — your business data stays yours.

  • End-to-end encrypted local database (SQLCipher)
  • Auto cloud sync & backup across devices (Pro)
  • Email/password + Google Sign-In authentication
  • Manage 99+ businesses from a single account (Pro)
How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No training needed. BizPocket is designed to be simple for any shopkeeper or small business owner.

1

Create your account

Sign up with your email or Google account. Verify with OTP and you're in — takes under a minute.

2

Add your business

Enter your business name, currency, and preferences. Add your inventory, vendors, and expenses to get started.

3

Start managing

Record your first sale, track stock movement, view reports — everything in one place from day one.
